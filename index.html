Services

Landscape Install

Our landscape install service transforms your outdoor spaces into functional and aesthetically pleasing environments. It begins with sod cutting to remove grass from designated bed areas, followed by adding soil amendments to enhance the quality of the soil for optimal plant growth. We then plant flowers, shrubs, or other greenery tailored to your design preferences. To complete the installation, we add a top layer of mulch or decorative stone, for visual appeal.

Mulching

Our mulching service focuses on maintaining the health and appearance of your garden beds. In the spring, we clean up any debris or deteriorated mulch from the previous season and apply a fresh two inch layer of mulch to enrich the soil and improve curb appeal. In the fall, we lightly dust over the existing mulch to refresh its look and ensure continued protection for the soil through the colder months. Leaf cleanup service ensures your property remains neat and free of debris throughout the year.

Popups and Sump Pumps

This ervice is designed to protect your home's foundation by effectively managing water drainage. We install popup extensions that divert water from downspouts about ten feet away from your house, preventing pooling and potential damage. Additionally, we can integrate sump pumps systems with similar ten foot extensions, complete with cleanouts and freeze guards, ensuring functionality in all weather conditions.

Edge Stone

Our edge stone service enchances the borders of your garden beds with durable and visually appealing designs. We install decorative edge stones that add a unique touch to your landscaping and secure them with concrete to ensure stability and long lasting performance. With our trash enclosures, we help to keep your property organized and visually appealing by creating a 4x6 fenched area specifically designed to conceal trash cans. It is built with a durable paver base to ensure a stable foundation.
